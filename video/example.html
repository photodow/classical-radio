Description

Notice that Troy was not afraid to mix static imagery (using Ken Burns effects) with video material. Shorter clips tend to be more effective in attracting attention especially with upbeat audio support, generally a 4 second default is advised depending on the tempo. Focus on the quality of your work not the quantity. Transitions should only be used when applicable such as location changes or a significant change in the story line of your video. Remember that every Premiere property that has a stop watch is something that can be key framed ( animated ). Having said that, keep in mind the intent of your effects as they affect your video, don't simply use them without reason.
