"Welcome to Fullsail!"

A video montage of FullSail University's campus (including the sign, students walking around, teachers, and classes in session) is shown with "space" music playing in the background. This advertisement is geared toward adults who are serious about receiving a degree involving technology and design in a quick and efficient manner.
