"Why spend another day in heavy traffic when you could be relaxing on a Florida beach in Paradise?"

This infomercial features a video montage of Florida's sparkling beaches, sunsets, sail boats, and other fun activities. While these images (probably shot in the 90's) are shown, intense early 90's techno music plays in the background (ironic for this classical music radio station, unless 90's music is now considered classical by the youth of today). This commercial certainly brings Florida's beaches to life with a splash which lines up perfectly with the music.
